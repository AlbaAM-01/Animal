Pudu

The smallest deer.The name is a loanword from Mapudungun, the language of the indigenous Mapuche people of central Chile and south-western Argentina.

Taxonomy

Pudus are classified in the New World deer subfamily Capreolinae within the deer family Cervidae.
Two similar species of pudu are recognised:

  1. Pudu puda
  2. Pudu mephistophiles

Habitat

The pudu inhabits temperature reainforests in South America, where the dense underbrush and bamboo thickets offer protection from predators.
We can found in:

  • Venezuela
  • Colombia
  • Ecuador
  • Peru
